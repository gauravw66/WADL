Career Highlights

Rohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer widely recognized for his elegant batting and ability to score big centuries, especially in limited-overs formats.

  • 3-time ICC Cricket World Cup winner (2007, 2011, 2013)
  • 5-time IPL Champion as captain of Mumbai Indians
  • Holder of the record for the highest individual score in an ODI (264 runs)
  • 3-time ICC Champions Trophy winner
