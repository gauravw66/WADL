About Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma, born on April 30, 1987, in Bansod, India, is one of the most prolific cricketers of the modern era. Known for his elegant stroke play and ability to score big centuries, he has been an essential part of the Indian cricket team. Rohit is the only player to have scored three double centuries in One Day Internationals (ODIs), a testament to his ability to dominate in limited-overs cricket.

Early Life and Career

Rohit Sharma's journey to the national team was not easy, but his determination and talent shone through. He initially started as a middle-order batsman before being promoted to open the innings. This move proved to be a turning point in his career, and he has since become one of the most successful openers in international cricket.

Achievements

  • 3-time ICC Cricket World Cup Winner (2007, 2011, 2013)
  • 3-time ICC Champions Trophy winner
  • 5-time Indian Premier League (IPL) Champion as captain of Mumbai Indians
  • Holder of the record for the highest individual score in an ODI match (264 runs)
