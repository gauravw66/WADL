About Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma, born on April 30, 1987, in Bansod, India, is one of the most talented cricketers of the modern era. Known for his elegant batting and ability to score big centuries, especially in limited-overs formats, he has earned accolades across all formats of the game. As the captain of the Mumbai Indians, he has led his team to multiple IPL titles.

Early Life and Career

Rohit's cricket journey began at a young age. Initially, he struggled to find his footing, but his breakthrough came in 2007 when he was selected for the Indian national team. Since then, his career has been marked by consistency, remarkable leadership, and memorable performances on the field.

Achievements

  • 3-time ICC Cricket World Cup winner (2007, 2011, 2013)
  • 5-time IPL Champion as captain of Mumbai Indians
  • Holder of the record for the highest individual score in an ODI (264 runs)
  • 3-time ICC Champions Trophy winner
